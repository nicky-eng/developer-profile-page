About me

After graduating in Mechanical Engineering I worked in the OCTG industry and later I was a full-time head teaching assistant at the Mechanical Engineering department at my alma mater for a period of over 6 years. During this time I started programming in MATLAB and Arduino for work and also as a hobby.

While learning Python and web technologies for IOT devices I discovered a passion for programming, so when COVID forced us into quarantine in March 2020, and all things remote, I began an Algorithms and Data Structures course and 3 months later I got my first developer job as a Django Developer.

In December 2020 I quit my teaching job and became a full time developer. I am now concentrating primarily in backend development using Python and Django.
